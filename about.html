Massage by Referral Only: A Personalized Approach to Wellness

At the heart of my mobile massage practice is a simple philosophy: healing thrives in trust. That’s why I work exclusively by referral.

This isn’t just about exclusivity—it’s about creating a space where every client arrives through a trusted connection. Whether you were referred by a friend, a fellow wellness practitioner, or someone whose life was touched by our work together, you’re already part of a circle built on care, respect, and intention.

Working by referral allows me to:

  • Offer deeply personalized sessions tailored to your emotional and physical needs

  • Maintain a safe, respectful environment for both client and therapist

  • Focus on quality over quantity, ensuring each visit supports your holistic well-being.

Meet the Therapist

I specialize in delivering first-class restorative treatments. I aim to nurture and relax each and every client with individualized and innovative therapeutic techniques. Creating a safe place for healing to occur, I use natural and pure ingredients to nourish your body and soul. My treatments are specifically tailored to the needs of each client to make sure they receive the approach that works best for them. Time and again I have seen the incredible healing power of my treatments. 

The care of my clients is always my first priority.  That includes truly listening to their needs and wants before, during, and after their session; not only what they tell me but what their bodies tell me as well.  My work is an intuitive blend of many modalities and styles aimed at suiting those needs the best way I can.  I have had specialized training in chronic pain relief and have great success over the years greatly decreasing pain intensity in my clients.  My skin-care treatments are completely customized according to each skin type for every client of all ages. Whether you are dealing with sensitivity or stubborn acne, or just want to maintain the glowing appearance of your skin, simply do not hesitate to treat the beautiful appearance of your skin. ​ ​ ​
